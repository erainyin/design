Discovery

Every project should start with a clear user - and market-driven strategy. But to do that, you have to have a deep understanding of the market you're dealing with. And be clear about what the project is trying to achieve. This is why we need to explore and research first -- only based on this can we analyze the market, research competitive products, identify target users and core demands and then define the design goal.

back

UX Design

Based on the positioning of products and brands, we can start to design interactive products. Starting from the core functions of the product, all the pages to be designed (Wireframe) are sorted out. Then, through operation guidance, the relationship between pages is established to realize a complete operation process (LoFi interactive Prototype). How to make your product pages work more smoothly. Completing the functions provided by the page is the key concern of interaction design.

back

UI Design

Design the page visually with the prototype of interaction design. Carry on the design style discussion and the research, the choice tone, the font and so on. Consider visual, usability, and development costs. Design the front-end UI. From the most granular elements to reusable components. Then extended to the overall adjustment of cross-platform and cross-device. Produce a complete UI design scheme.
